Common Questions

How do I find emails from a specific person?

Use the "From (Sender)" filter in Advanced Search, or type their name in the main search box.

What are the different views?

By Date: Shows emails in chronological order.
By Sender: Groups emails by who sent them.
By Thread: Groups related emails together.

How do I filter by date?

Click "Advanced Search" and select a date range. You can use quick presets like "This Week" or choose custom dates.
